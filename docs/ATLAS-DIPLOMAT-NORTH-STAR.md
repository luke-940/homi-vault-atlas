# Atlas Diplomat North Star

## Mission

v7.5 created the space and stage. v7.6 puts real protagonists, relations, time, and operational meaning into that stage. Atlas Builder must understand the Vault deeply enough to translate the agent world into an experience that a person without Vault context can recognize, understand, and trust.

The reader journey is fixed:

1. **Three seconds — attention.** MOC, Papers, Signals, and at least one evidence-backed protagonist are visible.
2. **Thirty seconds — understanding.** The reader can explain why the protagonist matters and distinguish incoming from outgoing relations.
3. **Three minutes — conviction.** The reader can trace verified change and understand how Luke and sovereign owners contribute without confusing responsibility with command.

## Evidence grammar

- Position remains structural: X district, Y meaningful freshness, Z structural depth.
- Color remains district identity. Homi amber remains committed selection or finite verified activity.
- Node radius and bounded local light remain monotonic with `uniqueInboundDocuments`.
- `inboundLinkOccurrences`, cross-domain reach, directed bridge centrality, meaningful freshness, and verified change are separate advisory metrics. They are never collapsed into a single score.
- A knowledge line is an actual directed `references` edge from `atlas.graph.v1`.
- A verified handoff is a dated semantic movement with its own grammar. It is not a knowledge edge.
- An operational alignment is responsibility, stewardship, circulation, translation, or observation. It is not a command or approval edge.

## Atlas Builder judgment

Metrics nominate a bounded candidate set. Atlas Builder reads the candidate source, its strongest incoming and outgoing neighbors, and its cross-domain role before choosing a protagonist. Each choice records a Korean thesis, supporting metrics, evidence references, confidence, caveat, and public-safety decision.

MOC, Papers, and Signals are not scenery. MOC binds durable knowledge, Papers supplies evidence gateways, and Signals holds verified domains and storylines of change. If any core domain lacks a defensible protagonist, the product states the coverage gap instead of inventing one.

## Visual and motion contract

Preserve the v7.5 warm graphite cosmic field, perspective Canvas2D, node-owned halo, editorial rail, and factual route choreography. The background supports spatial orientation; it is not a fake star field. Home remains full-viewport and unobstructed.

Motion verbs are `reveal`, `focus`, `trace`, `compare`, and `align`. Entry settles once within 800 ms. Focus transitions stay within 420–560 ms and bounded camera movement. Reduced motion removes translation, zoom, and line drawing while preserving the final evidence state.

### v7.5 inheritance and the Homi core

The praised v7.5 result did not succeed because it imitated a literal planet. It succeeded because spatial depth, restrained typography, node-owned illumination, negative space, and a small number of factual relation traces formed one coherent stage. v7.6 must preserve those authored qualities.

The Homi center is a modern stellar core and provenance anchor, not a knowledge metric or a decorative planet. It must use the real Homi mark with restrained, integrated local light. Glossy metallic spheres, Saturn-like rings, faux planetary material, beveled badges, fake orbital dots, and `SYSTEM ANCHOR` decoration are rejected visual regressions. They read as early-2000s science-fiction ornament and must not return.

Homi remains visually central without becoming the biggest graph node or acquiring synthetic edges. MOC, Papers, Signals, and evidence-backed protagonists carry the factual graph meaning around it.
